# Introduction to SAP Plant Maintenance (PM)

**SAP Plant Maintenance (PM)** is an enterprise module designed to manage all equipment and facility maintenance activities within an organization.

It helps companies:

- Track and manage assets  
- Prevent unplanned equipment failures  
- Reduce overall maintenance and operating costs  

This is achieved by automating key maintenance processes such as:

- Inspections  
- Notifications  
- Corrective repairs  
- Preventive maintenance schedules  

---

## Key Benefits

### Reduced Downtime
Companies using SAP PM can achieve up to a **92% reduction in equipment downtime**, ensuring continuous operations.

---

### Asset Lifecycle Extension
- Increases the lifespan of critical machinery and infrastructure  
- Promotes proactive maintenance  

---

### Resource Optimization
- Improves efficiency in managing:
  - Maintenance technicians  
  - Spare parts and inventory  

---

## Summary

- SAP PM centralizes **maintenance management and asset tracking**  
- Enables **preventive and corrective maintenance automation**  
- Delivers significant improvements in:
  - Equipment uptime  
  - Asset longevity
